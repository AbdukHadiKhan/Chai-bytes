About Us

At Chai & Bytes Café, we believe that the best ideas are brewed over a hot cup of chai. Nestled just outside the college gates, our café is more than a place to grab a quick snack — it’s a cozy hub where students come together to relax, brainstorm, and share stories. With our signature masala chai, refreshing coffees, and tasty homemade snacks, we aim to create a warm space that feels like home while fueling both minds and conversations.
